## Logo description

- **Shape:** Circular badge
- **Tree:** Large, detailed oak silhouette in deep forest green filling the upper half of the circle
- **Climber figure:** Warm bronze/gold tree surgeon silhouette rappelling from the trunk
- **Roots:** Visible root system in dark green extending below the name
- **Outer ring:** Two concentric rings — inner dark green, outer warm gold
- **Name:** LITTLE JOES in bold dark forest green capital letters
- **Sub-text:** TREE SERVICES in warm bronze/gold capital letters
- **Style:** Heritage-inspired, detailed, premium trade brand

## Typography guidance

- **Headings:** Modern, premium weight — suggested: Playfair Display, Fraunces, or similar serif with character
- **Body:** Clean, highly readable sans-serif — suggested: Inter, DM Sans, or similar
- **No rustic/distressed fonts** — the logo carries the heritage feel; the UI should feel modern
- **No display fonts that look AI-generic or template-ish**

Final font choice is at developer/designer discretion but must align with the premium countryside-professional feel.

---

## Logo usage rules

- Always use the transparent version on coloured or photographic backgrounds
- Always use the white background version for documents, email footers, and OG images
- Do not stretch, distort, recolour, or apply effects to the logo
- Minimum display size: 120px wide for digital use
- Do not place the logo on backgrounds that clash with the green/gold palette
- Maintain clear space around the logo equal to approximately the height of the letter J in JOES
